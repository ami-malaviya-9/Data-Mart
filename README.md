# Data Mart Analysis in SQL

## SCHEMA USED: WEEKLY_SALES TABLE
This project analyzes sales performance for Data Mart, focusing on the impact of a major operational change introduced in June 2020, when the company switched to sustainable packaging across its supply chain.

The analysis explores trends in sales, transactions, customer segments, demographics, and platforms to quantify how this change affected business performance.

This case study demonstrates SQL skills in data cleaning, transformation, and exploratory analysis.

## Objectives

Clean and transform raw weekly sales data

Engineer meaningful business features

Explore trends across regions, platforms, and customer segments

Evaluate the impact of the June 2020 operational change

Identify key contributors to sales performance

## Exploratory Analysis Questions

The project answers the following business questions:

### A. Data Coverage

Which week numbers are missing from the dataset?

How many total transactions occurred each year?

### B. Sales Performance

Total sales by region per month

Platform-wise transaction counts

Monthly sales distribution between Retail and Shopify

Sales percentage by demographic each year

### C. Customer Insights

Which age bands and demographics contribute most to Retail sales?
